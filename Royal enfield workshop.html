Royal Enfield Workshop

November 2023

SAE DTU orchestrated an exceptional workshop, forging a collaborative alliance with the renowned motorcycle giant of India, Royal Enfield. This event proved to be an enriching experience for participants, offering profound insights into the intricacies of the 2-wheelers sector. Through a well-curated program, students had the unique opportunity to delve into the nuances of the industry, gaining valuable industrial knowledge that extends beyond the confines of traditional classroom learning.





The inclusion of a hands-on component elevated the workshop's impact, allowing students to not only absorb theoretical concepts but also to engage actively with the subject matter. The pinnacle of this immersive experience was the chance to ride the newly launched Royal Enfield Hunter motorcycle. This firsthand encounter with cutting-edge technology and design must have provided an unparalleled understanding of the practical aspects of the field. In essence, the workshop served as a bridge between academia and industry, offering a holistic learning experience. The collaboration with a leading brand like Royal Enfield not only brought credibility to the event but also ensured that participants were exposed to the latest trends and innovations in the dynamic world of 2-wheelers. Such initiatives play a pivotal role in shaping well-rounded professionals, preparing them for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in their academic and professional jourey.
