CAD Workshop

October 2023

SAE DTU organized a hands-on workshop where students had the opportunity to learn and practice their CAD skills using software such as CATIA, Solidworks, ANSYS, AutoCAD, and more.





The workshop provided them with a deeper understanding of the design process and allowed them to explore the endless possibilities that CAD technology offers in various industries. Through this workshop, students were able to enhance their design skills and gain valuable experience that will benefit them in their future endeavors.
