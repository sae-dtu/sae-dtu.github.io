Robotics Workshop

July 2023

SAE's commitment to bridging the gap in robotics education in government schools has proven to be a resounding success.





Through the dedication of our student trainers and the enthusiasm of the participating students, we have not only imparted practical knowledge but have also sowed the seeds for a future where technology is harnessed to drive innovation and progress. The impact of SAE's robotics training initiative extended beyond the immediate acquisition of skills. Students developed a keen interest in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields, paving the way for a future generation of innovators and technologists. The initiative also fostered a sense of community and collaboration among the participating schools, creating a positive ripple effect in the local educational ecosystem.
