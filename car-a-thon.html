Car-a-thon

12 Aug 2022

Car-a-thon was a quiz conducted by the SAE DTU during Invictus to test participants' knowledge of automobiles.The quiz covered a wide range of topics, including automotive technology, design, manufacturing, and history.Participants were requiredto answer a series of questions related to these topics, with the fastest and most accurate participants progressing to the later rounds of the competition.





Prizes worth Rs. 10,000 were also given to the winners.
