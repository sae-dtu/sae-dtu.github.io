HYDROJET

12 Aug 2022

In this hydro jet event, each team had to prepare a launcher in which they had to launch a water bottle as a rocket by increasing pressure inside the bottle. It was a cross-college event consisting of 2 rounds:





Round 1 : All the material will be given to the team and they have to figure out the launcher's design.Each team will design the launcher on the sheet first then only the actual prototype can be made. Points will be awarded on the following basis of structural stability, pressure holding capacity and design. Round 2: Each team will be given 2 chances to launch the rocket. Points will be allocated based on the Maximum height and distance travelled by the bottle (rocket).
