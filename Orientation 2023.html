Orientation 2023

2nd September 2023

Orientation for the recruitment of 1st and 2nd year students held on 2nd September at OAT to introduce the society and its activities to the new and prospective members.





The orientation was attended by students of different branches and batches. The orientation program consisted of an overview of the society, its vision, mission and goals, followed by the presentation of the current and past projects and the future of the society. The orientation also featured a session on the benefits and opportunities of joining the society, such as learning new skills, gaining practical experience, networking with industry experts, and participating in national and international competitions.
